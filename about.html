My Story

Founded in 2012 by Nwaosu Livinus Chukwudi, STEPLICHN Construction Company began as a small team of dedicated builders with a vision to transform Nigeria's construction landscape. What started as humble projects in Owerri has grown into a trusted name across Imo State and neighboring regions.

From residential homes to large commercial complexes, my portfolio reflects a commitment to quality and innovation. I have successfully completed over 50 projects, earning accolades for my craftsmanship and customer satisfaction.

my journey has been marked by a commitment to quality that goes beyond industry standards. Each project i undertake reflects my core belief that buildings should be both structurally sound and aesthetically remarkable.
